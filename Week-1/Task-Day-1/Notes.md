# 📘 Salesforce Task Day 1 Notes

## What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform used by companies to manage customers, sales, services, and business operations.

### Uses of Salesforce
- Store customer information
- Track sales and leads
- Manage customer support
- Automate business workflows
- Generate reports and analytics

---

## What is CRM?

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.

CRM helps businesses:
- Manage customer interactions
- Improve communication
- Store customer data
- Increase productivity

---

## Why Companies Use Salesforce

Companies use Salesforce because:
- It is cloud-based
- Easy to access from anywhere
- Improves customer management
- Provides automation features
- Helps sales teams work efficiently

### Companies Using Salesforce

## Salesforce Objects and Records

### Object
An Object is a database table used to store data in Salesforce.

Examples:
- Account
- Contact
- Lead
- Opportunity

---

### Record
A Record is a single entry inside an object.

Example:

| Name | Phone |
|---|---|
| Rahul | 9876543210 |

---

## Types of Objects

### Standard Objects
Objects already available in Salesforce.

Examples:
- Account
- Contact
- Lead

---

### Custom Objects
Objects created according to business requirements.

Examples:
- Student Object
- Employee Object

---

## Salesforce Developer

A Salesforce Developer:
- Builds applications
- Writes Apex code
- Creates automation
- Develops Lightning Components

---

## Difference Between Admin and Developer

| Salesforce Admin | Salesforce Developer |
|---|---|
| Manages settings | Develops applications |
| Uses configuration | Writes code |
| Creates reports | Builds automation |
